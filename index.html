Verdicts

Five outcomes.

✅ ALIGNED
Consistent with tree.
🆕 NEW_TERRITORY
Tree has no guidance.
❓ NEEDS_REVIEW
Partial overlap. Human required.
⚠️ CONFLICT
Contradicts a tree decision.
❔ INSUFFICIENT
Tree info too thin to decide.

Two modes

Your repo. Or someone else's.

Maintainer

Install gardener in your own repo.

target_repo: acme/api

External reviewer

Review an OSS project you don't maintain. Config lives in your tree repo.

target_repo: paperclipai/paperclip
tree_repo:   github.com/you/tree
config_repo: you/tree
